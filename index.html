A Little Bit About Me

I am currently studying Computer Science and Data Science at UC Berkeley and am actively seeking internships and similar opportunities. On top of building meaningful projects, I am passionate about mentorship and advocating for women advancement in STEM on campus. As a consultant for the Data Science Department and an academic intern for the Computer Science Department, I strive to use my knowledge to support others in their endeavors. I actively work with my peers at the Association of Women in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Phi Sigma Rho, and other communities like NCWIT and RTC Fellows. Outside of academics, I am also interested in photography, guitar, painting, and dancing.
